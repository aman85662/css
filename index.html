Royal Bakery

Royal Cafe is a charming and inviting spot perfect for anyone looking to enjoy a relaxing time. Located in the heart of Bakshi Ka Talab, Uttar Pradesh, this cafe offers a delightful blend of traditional and modern ambiance.
As you step inside, you're greeted by warm, cozy interiors adorned with elegant decor that reflects a royal touch. The aroma of freshly brewed coffee and baked goods fills the air, making it an ideal place for a casual meeting, a study session, or simply unwinding with a good book.
The menu at Royal Cafe is diverse, featuring a variety of beverages including rich coffees, refreshing teas, and delicious smoothies. For food lovers, the cafe offers an array of mouth-watering snacks, delectable desserts, and hearty meals. Their signature dishes, such as the Royal Chaat and the special Masala Tea, are a must-try.

Delite Cafe

Delite Cafe is a cozy and welcoming spot, perfect for enjoying a delightful meal or a refreshing drink. Established in 2013 by two childhood friends in Hamtramck, Delite Cafe has grown to become a beloved neighborhood gem1. The cafe offers a wide variety of delicious options, including subs, wraps, soups, salads, fresh smoothies, specialty coffees, and delectable desserts1. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty breakfast, a light lunch, or a sweet treat, Delite Cafe has something to satisfy every craving.
